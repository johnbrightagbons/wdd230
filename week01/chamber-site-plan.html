Benin Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Benin Chamber of Commerce - The site name includes the word "Chamber" to clearly convey the organization's identity. It is relevant to the locale (Benin) where the chamber operates, representing its location and purpose as a regional business association.

Site Purpose

Promote Local Businesses: The website aims to promote and support local businesses in Carlsbad by providing information on member services, networking events, and economic development initiatives.

Community Engagement: The website serves as a platform for community engagement, fostering collaboration and partnerships between local businesses and residents. It provides information on community events and public policy advocacy.

Scenarios (Visitor Questions)

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the Benin area?

Color Schema

Primary Color (Blue): The primary color, a shade of blue, represents professionalism and trust. It will be used for headings, buttons, and accents throughout the site.

Secondary Color (Green): The secondary color, a shade of green, symbolizes growth and community. It will be used for background elements, highlighting, and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

Font (Primary): "Roboto" will be used as the primary font for the website. It provides a clean and modern look.

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular

Font (Secondary, for special sections): "Arial" will be used sparingly for specific sections where a different style is desired.
